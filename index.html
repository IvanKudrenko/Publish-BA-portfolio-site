About

I care about technology because the best products feel calm, useful, and deeply intentional.

I am Ivan Kudrenko. I have been interested in devices, apps, interfaces, and future technology since early childhood. I was never only interested in using technology. I kept thinking about how it could feel better, look better, and solve the right problem in a more thoughtful way.

That is why BA matters to me. I came up with the name when I was 8, and it stayed with me because it felt like the right home for the products I wanted to build over time. Today, I am shaping that direction through real work: ActiView, BlinkClip, design thinking, hardware experiments, and public creative work.

I studied in Ukraine at a Waldorf school, where creativity and independent thinking were taken seriously. Later, at Andrews Osborne Academy, I kept developing my direction in a broader academic environment.
