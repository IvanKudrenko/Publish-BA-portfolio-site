Why I Publish

I want the process to be visible, not only the finished result.

What the channel is for

A public place for the thinking behind the products.

My YouTube work gives the site and the products a public layer. It lets me share ideas earlier, test how they sound in the real world, and build a clearer record of what I care about.

Why it matters to me

I do not want everything to stay private until it looks perfect

Some ideas become stronger when they are shared before they are completely finished, especially when the work is part product direction, part experiment, and part creative process.

What this adds to the site

Public proof of the founder side of the work

The channel helps people understand that the site is not just a static portfolio. It is part of an active body of work that is still growing.

Content Direction

The channel is meant to reflect the same interests that drive BA and my product work.

Technology ideas

Where I think products should go

I want the channel to be a place where I can talk about the kind of technology experiences I believe should exist.

Experiments

Real tests, not only opinions

Experiments matter because they show the work in motion, whether that means interface tests, product tries, or technical exploration.

Reviews

What I notice in other products

Reviews are useful because they reveal what I value in great products and what I think is missing in the current market.

Creative projects

The broader public side of what I make

Not every useful idea fits neatly into one category, so the channel gives me room to share the broader creative side of the work too.

How It Connects

YouTube matters here because it makes the founder story and the product story feel more alive.

For BA

Public identity

BA should not only exist as a logo or a page on this site. The public work helps turn it into something people can actually follow.

For products

Context around the build process

Product pages explain what I am building. YouTube can show the surrounding process, experiments, and thinking that do not fit into one page section.

For trust

More than static copy

Public work creates another layer of credibility because it shows that the ideas are connected to an active voice and an active process.

For the future

A place where the story can keep growing

As the products become stronger, the channel can grow with them instead of staying frozen as a single launch moment.
